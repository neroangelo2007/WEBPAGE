Do's in Iraqi culture

1. Greet respectfully

Use greetings like “As-salamu alaykum” and be polite when meeting others.

2. Show hospitality

Guests are highly valued—accepting food or drinks is polite.

3. Use your right hand

Always use your right hand for eating and giving items.

4. Dress modestly

Clothing should be respectful and not too revealing.

5. Respect elders

Give priority and speak politely to older individuals.

Don'ts in Iraqi culture

1. Don’t use your left hand

The left hand is considered unclean in social situations.

2. Don’t show public affection

Public displays of affection are not socially acceptable.

3. Don’t criticize religion

Religion is very important and should be respected.

4. Don’t dress immodestly

Revealing clothing is considered inappropriate.

5. Don’t refuse hospitality rudely

Always decline politely to avoid offending the host.
