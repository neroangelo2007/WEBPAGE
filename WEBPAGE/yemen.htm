Do's in Yemeni culture

1. Greet respectfully

Use polite greetings like “As-salamu alaykum” and show respect when meeting others.

2. Dress modestly

Wear clothing that covers the body appropriately in public.

3. Use your right hand

Always use your right hand when eating or giving items.

4. Accept hospitality

Guests are highly valued—accepting food or drinks is polite.

5. Respect elders and traditions

Show politeness and follow traditional customs in social situations.

Don'ts in Yemeni culture

1. Don’t use your left hand

The left hand is considered unclean in social situations.

2. Don’t show public affection

Public displays of affection are not acceptable.

3. Don’t dress immodestly

Revealing clothing is considered disrespectful.

4. Don’t criticize religion

Religion is deeply respected and should not be criticized.

5. Don’t refuse hospitality rudely

Always decline politely to avoid offending the host.
