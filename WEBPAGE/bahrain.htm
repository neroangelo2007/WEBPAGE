Do's in Bahraini culture

1. Greet respectfully

Use greetings like “As-salamu alaykum” and be polite when meeting others.

2. Dress modestly

Wear appropriate clothing that covers shoulders and knees in public.

3. Use your right hand

Always use your right hand for eating and giving items.

4. Accept hospitality

It is polite to accept food, coffee, or tea offered by your host.

5. Respect elders

Show politeness and give priority to older individuals.

Don'ts in Bahraini culture

1. Don’t use your left hand

The left hand is considered unclean in social situations.

2. Don’t show public affection

Public displays of affection are not socially acceptable.

3. Don’t dress in revealing clothes

Immodest clothing is considered disrespectful.

4. Don’t criticize religion or leadership

These are sensitive topics and should be avoided.

5. Don’t refuse hospitality rudely

Always decline politely to avoid offending the host.
