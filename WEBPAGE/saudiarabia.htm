Do's in Saudi Arabian culture

1. Dress modestly

Wear clothing that covers the body appropriately, respecting local customs.

2. Use the right hand

Always use your right hand when eating, greeting, or giving items.

3. Show respect for religion

Islam plays a central role, so respect prayer times and religious practices.

4. Accept hospitality

Guests are highly valued—accepting food or drinks is polite.

5. Greet properly

Use greetings like “As-salamu alaykum” to show respect.

Don'ts in Saudi Arabian culture

1. Don’t use your left hand

The left hand is considered unclean for eating or giving.

2. Don’t show public affection

Public displays of affection are not socially acceptable.

3. Don’t criticize religion

Religion is deeply respected and should not be criticized.

4. Don’t dress immodestly

Revealing clothing is considered inappropriate.

5. Don’t interrupt prayer times

Businesses may pause during prayer, so be respectful.
