Do's in Qatar culture

1. Dress modestly

Wear clothing that covers shoulders and knees, especially in public places.

2. Use your right hand

Always use your right hand for eating and giving items.

3. Respect religion and traditions

Islam plays a major role, so respect prayer times and customs.

4. Accept hospitality

It is polite to accept food, coffee, or tea when offered.

5. Greet respectfully

Use greetings like “As-salamu alaykum” to show politeness.

Don'ts in Qatar culture

1. Don’t show public affection

Public displays of affection are not acceptable.

2. Don’t dress in revealing clothes

Wearing short or tight clothing can be considered disrespectful.

3. Don’t criticize religion or leadership

These are sensitive topics and should be avoided.

4. Don’t use your left hand

The left hand is considered unclean in social settings.

5. Don’t eat or drink publicly during Ramadan (daytime)

It is disrespectful to eat or drink in public while others are fasting.
