Do's in North Korean culture

1. Show respect to national leaders

Displaying respect toward leaders and their images is very important in daily life.

2. Follow rules and instructions strictly

Obeying laws and guidelines is expected at all times.

3. Dress modestly and neatly

People are expected to dress conservatively and appropriately in public.

4. Participate in community activities

Group activities and events are an important part of social life.

5. Show discipline in public behavior

Being orderly and respectful in public spaces is highly valued.

Don'ts in North Korean culture

1. Don’t disrespect leaders or their images

Any form of disrespect toward leadership is taken very seriously.

2. Don’t ignore national rules

Breaking laws or regulations can lead to serious consequences.

3. Don’t wear overly revealing or flashy clothing

Fashion is expected to remain simple and modest.

4. Don’t act individually in ways that disrupt the group

Strong emphasis is placed on collective behavior rather than individualism.

5. Don’t criticize the government openly

Public criticism is not allowed and should be avoided.
